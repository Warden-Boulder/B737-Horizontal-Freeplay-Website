About The Toolbox


This toolbox is desigened to assist aircraft mechanics while working across various airframes. It's intended as a supplemental aid and doesn't replace any required tooling, manuals, or procedures definee by official documentation or company SOPs.

The project originally began as a Python-based program developed while working on both Boeing 737NG and 737 MAX aircraft. Over time, it evolved into a web-based toolkit to make these utilies more accessible and easier to use in real-world environments.
